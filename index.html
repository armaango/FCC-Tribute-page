The Devil of Old Trafford

Sir Alex Fergusson with the spoils of 98-99 season: The European treble

Here is how he reigned over Manchester, the EPL and Europe :

  • Joined Manchester United as Manager in 1986
  • Longest serving Manchester United Manager - served for 26 years
  • Total trophy count - 38
  • Premier League Titles - 13 First title win in 92-93
  • FA Cup Titles - 5
  • Champions League Titles - 2
  • Most memorable season - The treble win of 98-99
  • Knighted by the Queen in 1999
  • Retired in 2013 as the most successful manager ever to have graced the Premier League

He had that skill of [knowing] have you got to put your arm round someone or have you got to lose your temper to get the best out of them. I remember him having a go at me at half-time and I had the sort of attitude that ‘Right, I’ll show him’. And I played well in the second half, so then he quickly knew how I would respond to him losing his temper. That followed me for the next 20 years, so it was a big mistake early on.

Ryan Giggs, Longest serving Player of Manchester United
